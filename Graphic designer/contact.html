Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with me

What is your design process like?

My design process typically involves discovery, research, conceptualization, design development, refinement, and final delivery. I believe in a collaborative approach, keeping clients involved throughout the process to ensure the final product meets their needs and exceeds expectations.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A simple logo might take 2-3 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity could take 6-8 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific project requirements.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need information about your business/project, target audience, competitors, project goals, design preferences, and any existing brand materials. I provide a detailed questionnaire to help gather this information efficiently.

Do you offer website design and development?

Yes, I offer website design services and work with trusted development partners to bring those designs to life. Whether you need a simple portfolio site or a more complex e-commerce platform, I can help create a digital presence that aligns with your brand and meets your business objectives.

What are your payment terms?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payment schedules. I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or credit card.

Do you work with clients internationally?

I work with clients from all over the world. Communication is handled via email, video calls, and project management tools to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of time zones or geographic locations.
