Neon Dreams

Branding & Identity

About the Project

Neon Dreams is a nightclub brand that needed a vibrant, energetic identity to capture the essence of their late-night experience. The design approach focused on creating a visual language that evokes the feeling of neon lights, urban nightlife, and electronic music.

The brand identity includes a dynamic logo system, typography, color palette, pattern design, and various applications including signage, merchandise, and digital assets.
