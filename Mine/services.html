Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, I offer maintenance packages that include updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support to keep your application running smoothly.

Can you work with my existing team?

I'm experienced in collaborating with existing development teams, designers, and project managers. I can integrate seamlessly into your workflow.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), but I'm also proficient in Vue.js, Python, Django, PostgreSQL, and various cloud platforms like AWS and Azure.
