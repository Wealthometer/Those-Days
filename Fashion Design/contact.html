Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer worldwide shipping with multiple options including standard (5-7 business days), express (2-3 business days), and overnight delivery. Free shipping is available on orders over $500.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unworn items with original tags. Custom orders and sale items are final sale. Return shipping is complimentary for defective items.

Do you offer custom sizing?

Yes, we offer custom sizing and alterations for most items. Please contact our customer service team to discuss your requirements and additional costs.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes, our Luxe Rewards program offers exclusive benefits including early access to collections, special discounts, and invitations to private events. Sign up is free with any purchase.
