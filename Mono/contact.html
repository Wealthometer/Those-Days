Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MonoWorld

We welcome submissions from talented 3D artists. Please send us your portfolio along with a brief description of your work to hello@monoworld.art. Our curation team reviews all submissions and will contact you if your work aligns with our aesthetic vision.

Usage rights vary by artist and model. Each 3D model in our gallery includes specific licensing information. For commercial use, please contact us directly to discuss licensing options and fees.

Yes! We regularly organize physical exhibitions in galleries and art spaces. We also create immersive VR experiences that allow visitors to interact with our 3D models in virtual environments. Check our newsletter for upcoming events.

Subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates about new 3D models, featured artists, and upcoming exhibitions. You can also follow us on social media for daily inspiration and behind-the-scenes content.

MonoWorld is the first gallery dedicated exclusively to monochromatic 3D art. Our focus on black and white aesthetics allows viewers to appreciate pure form, texture, and composition without the distraction of color. We also provide an immersive web experience with smooth animations and interactive 3D viewers.
